Mahjong Diary Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 31, 2025

1. Information Collection

We collect the following information to provide better services:

  • Personal settings: Username, profile picture (uploaded by you)
  • Game records: Time, location, scores, opponents, game modes, etc.
  • Usage data: Feature usage frequency, preferences, statistics
  • Device information: Model, OS version, app version
  • Photo library access: For saving shared images to your album
  • Subscription info: Processed through App Store

All data is primarily stored locally on your device. We won't upload it to servers without your consent.

2. Purpose of Data Use

We use collected information for:

  • Core features: Game records, statistics, trend analysis
  • Personalization: Customized suggestions based on your usage
  • Improvements: Optimizing app performance and experience
  • Support: Responding to your feedback and inquiries
  • Subscriptions: Processing purchases and renewals
  • Sharing: Generating and saving shareable images
  • Security: Preventing fraud and abuse

3. Data Storage & Security

Storage methods:

  • Local storage: Most data stays on your device
  • Encryption: Using iOS encryption mechanisms
  • iCloud backup: Included if you enable iCloud
  • Secure transfer: HTTPS encryption for network transmission
  • Access control: Requires user authorization
  • Regular updates: We continually improve security measures

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Sharing policy:

  • We don't sell, rent or trade your personal information
  • Data may be shared only when:
    • You explicitly consent
    • Required by law or government request
    • To protect our legal rights
    • With Apple Inc. for subscription processing

Third-party services:

  • App Store: Handles subscription payments
  • System services: Photo library, notifications, etc.

5. Your Rights & Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your stored personal information
  • Modify: Change username, profile picture anytime
  • Delete: Remove specific records or all data
  • Export: Download your game records
  • Revoke: Withdraw permissions and stop data collection
  • Control: Manage photo access, notifications, etc.
  • Subscription: Manage via App Store settings

6. Subscriptions & Payments

Subscription details:

  • Processed through App Store - we don't access payment info
  • We only receive subscription status for feature unlocking
  • Auto-renewal: Manage in App Store settings
  • Restore purchases: Supported via App Store
  • Refunds: Handled by Apple following their policies
  • Price changes: You'll be notified in advance

7. Photos & Images

Image-related features:

  • Profile pictures: Selected from your library, stored only for display
  • Shared images: Saved to your album, deletable anytime
  • Permission control: Manage in device settings
  • Security: Uploaded images aren't used for other purposes
  • Local processing: All image handling occurs on your device

8. Children's Privacy

For children under 13:

  • Not specifically designed for children under 13
  • Parental supervision recommended
  • We'll delete any discovered child data immediately
  • Parents may contact us to request data removal
  • We comply with relevant laws protecting children

9. Data Retention & Deletion

Retention policy:

  • Local data: Remains until you delete it
  • App deletion: Removes all local data
  • Account deletion: Not applicable as data is local
  • Backups: Follow Apple's iCloud retention policies
  • Subscription data: Kept for reasonable period after expiration

10. Policy Updates

About updates:

  • Notifications: We'll alert you to major changes in-app
  • Effective immediately upon posting
  • Change logs: Detailed explanations provided
  • Continued use implies acceptance
  • Current version always available in-app

11. International Data Transfer

Cross-border data:

  • Primary storage: On your local device
  • Service may involve international transfer
  • We comply with applicable data protection laws
  • Appropriate security measures implemented
  • Using our service implies consent to possible transfers

12. Contact Us

For questions, suggestions or complaints:

  • Email: chenyisy@gmail.com
  • In-app feedback
  • Response time: Within 3 business days
  • Complaints: Taken seriously and addressed
  • Your feedback helps improve our privacy protections

Important Notice

Using this app means you accept all terms of this Privacy Policy. We're committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. If you disagree with any terms, please discontinue use.
